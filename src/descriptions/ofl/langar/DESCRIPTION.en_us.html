Langar has informal, playful letterforms and supports the Gurmukhi and Latin writing systems. It broadly follows the ‘upright italic’ Latin style, and introduces a new experimental style to the Gurmukhi script. This harmonized Latin-Gurmukhi design aims to expand the possibilities for everyone working with bilingual texts. It works best in larger sizes, providing a contrasting secondary style to a more traditional ‘text typeface’ style.

To contribute, see github.com/ammazzarella/Langar
